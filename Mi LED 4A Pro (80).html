a.Pre-set Video modes: Standard, Movie, Picture and Sports. Users can also customize the display parameters.

Mi TV supports 4000+ DTH/Cable Operators across India.

Mi TV comes with Google’s Play Store. You can find TV apps in the play store. These apps are specially made for TV. Please note: You will not find all the apps that you find on your phones on TV Play Store.

Yes, you can use Google’s Home app to mirror your screen and watch content from apps like Netflix and Prime video.
